Safety Guidelines

Practical safety tips and awareness strategies to help you stay protected in various situations.

👁️

Stay Alert & Aware

Always be conscious of your surroundings. Trust your instincts if something feels wrong.

📱

Share Your Location

Use location sharing with trusted contacts when traveling alone or in unfamiliar areas.

🚶‍♀️

Safe Travel Practices

Use well-lit routes, avoid isolated areas at night, and keep emergency contacts accessible.

🔒

Digital Safety

Protect your online presence with strong passwords and privacy settings on social media.

🏠

Home Security

Secure your home with proper locks, lighting, and avoid letting strangers know you're alone.

🤝

Build Support Networks

Maintain connections with family, friends, and community support groups.
